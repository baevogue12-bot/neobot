3 Before you begin

⚠️ Note: before you begin

  • Minimum capital: your account balance must be at least USD 1,000. Accounts below this amount will NOT be accepted.
  • MT5 account access: the EA service requires your MT5 account credentials. Your funds will ALWAYS remain held by your broker — we CANNOT withdraw, transfer, or deposit funds from your account. Only you can request withdrawals directly from your broker.

  • Keep the trade open for 15–30 seconds, then close it.
  • Make sure to do this during low volatility hours.
